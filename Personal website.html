About

Hi! I'm Luxury Exotics

Entrepreneur Designer Developer

BBT started in 2009 as a benchmark model for the Pre-Used, or how we prefer to see it as, Pre-Loved Car Brand. The mission was simple, direct and drove effect - delivering a new dimension of luxury while standardising & raising platforms for the used car market in India. Since our inception our primary aim has been our passion for delivering excellence which became our mission. YOU (our patrons) are the driving force behind our company and making sure you get the best is what we thrive on. If you are interested to control your own professional life and simultaneously enjoy freedom, freelancing is an attractive option for car enthusiasts. BBT will address you as BBT Squad as you actively work in your respective cities/ locations to purchase exotic pre-owned and brand new cars across Pan India, as per the BBT quality parameters. This model will work on a commission based structure as per the deal value. Every freelancer will be part of BBT Family with proper communication channel for active response system. Of course, staying on top of your deadlines should be a priority as well as prompt replies to clients would be required. Good communication skills are desirable. You need to be willing to have hard conversations such as negotiating a higher rate or breaking up with a client. Being tactful and professional would be advantageous as it helps create a pleasurable working experience with customers.
